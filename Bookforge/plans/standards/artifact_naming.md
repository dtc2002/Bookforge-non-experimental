# Artifact Naming Rules

Artifacts must follow predictable naming conventions.

## Story Planning

story_skeleton.json

beat_outline.json

chapter_plan.json

planning_assessment.json

## Scene Planning

scene_packets_chapter_<n>.json

## Drafting

draft_chapter_<n>.md

## Validation Reports

validation_chapter_<n>.json

## Versioning

Artifacts may include version numbers if revised.

Example:

story_skeleton_v2.json
chapter_plan_v3.json

Only one artifact version is marked as active in the project manifest.
